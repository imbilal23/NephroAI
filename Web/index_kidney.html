Model Architecture

Transparent Clinical Pipeline

A detailed breakdown of how NephroAI processes data, from raw clinical inputs to rigorous evaluation and final recommendations.

Clinical Feature Integration

Processes up to 24 clinical parameters, strictly analyzing 15 mandatory features (demographics, blood biochemistry) and gracefully handling 9 optional features.

→

KNN Data Imputation

Utilizes K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithms to intelligently estimate missing optional lab results by identifying the closest multidimensional patient matches.

→

Imputation-Aware Scaling

Utilizes dynamic StandardScaler pipelines specifically to optimize KNN distance calculations, ensuring high-fidelity estimation of missing optional clinical markers.

→

Ensemble Voting Architecture

Leverages a powerful Voting Classifier that mathematically aggregates predictions from Random Forest, XGBoost, Gradient Boosting, and AdaBoost models.

→

Rigorous Evaluation

Validated using rigorous Stratified K-Fold Cross-Validation, specifically optimizing for F1-Score to achieve an exceptional ~99% accuracy on imbalanced datasets.

→

Actionable Risk Outputs

Translates complex mathematical probabilities into clear binary risk assessments (CKD vs. Not CKD), paired with concrete clinical referral recommendations.

→
